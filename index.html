In-the-Wild Human-Object Interaction Motion Annotation

Boran Wen, Ye Lu, Keyan Wan, Yong-Lu Li • Shanghai Jiao Tong University • 2025.7

Introduction

In this work, we aim to annotate open-world, open-semantic human interactions with the lowest possible cost for the entire annotation process. We initially reconstruct people, objects, and spatial relationships using state-of-the-art 3D reconstruction tools. Subsequently, we employ our designed keypoint annotation system for click-based interactive annotation and utilize our HOISolver system to optimize and obtain the complete reconstruction results. We hope to collect various interaction video data across different object categories to provide a data foundation for us.

Our previous work focuses on image-level human-object interaction annotation, which is accepted at CVPR 2025. Our new work is still on progress, we will release our tools and data in the future.
